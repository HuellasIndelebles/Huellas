We present a new participatory and interactive forum, in telematic format as an instrument of connection and communication.
We have created a safe and inclusive space, suitable for all those romantics or not, who identify with the
common interest of tackling a motivating exercise and feel gratified with the writing and narration of events and adventures that have occurred
throughout their life.
Our ambition is to provide an environment in which you can share your distant memories, captured in sketches and reflections,
aimed at building from the past, what can embellish the present and create a legacy, as a bridge to the future.

Who we are

A group of friends, who agreed to call this adventure "INDELIBLE FOOTPRINTS",
where we pursue to encourage personal skills and competencies,
often hidden in some corner and that sprout as soon as they find fertile ground,
and that will surely serve as both personal help and motivation for others.

Founding Group

Goals

Promote activity, knowledge and relationships between people with similar sensibilities.
Open to participants without restriction of age, gender or condition.
